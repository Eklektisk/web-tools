Firefighting

One of the two crises the fire service is facing is a lack of members.

75% of all fire companies in the United States are volunteer companies. A lot of people appear to be under the misconception that most firefighters are paid. This could not be any farther from the truth.

Consider donating to your local fire company or volunteering your time! If you decide volunteering is right for you, contact your local fire company and they will be more than willing to address any questions or concerns you have. And of course, whether you choose to volunteer or make a donation, your local company will always be happy for the help!
